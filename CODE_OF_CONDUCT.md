# Code of Conduct

We follow the spirit of the Contributor Covenant to foster a welcoming, harassment-free community.

## Expectations

- Be respectful, inclusive, and collaborative.
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.
- Assume good intent while addressing impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive behavior that derails discussions or work.
- Publication of others' private information without consent.

## Reporting

Report incidents to the maintainers via the contact listed in `SECURITY.md`. Provide as much detail as possible so we can investigate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or removal from the community. Repeated or severe violations will not be tolerated.
